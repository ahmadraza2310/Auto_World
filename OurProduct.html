Suzuki Alto 2022 Hybrid

Engine
The 2022 Alto has a 1.0-liter K10C petrol engine with idle start/stop technology. The petrol motor has 66bhp and 89Nm of torque and is paired with a five-speed manual or an AMT unit.
Fuel
The Alto has a fuel average of 18 kilometers per liter (KM/L) and a fuel tank capacity of 27 liters.The petrol automatic variant is claimed to get 24.90 kmpl, while the petrol manual variant gets 24.39 kmpl.
Dimensions
The Alto is 3395 mm long, 1475 mm wide, and 1490 mm high, and has a seating capacity of four people.
Features
The 2022 Alto Standard has manual door mirrors, manual headlamps, manual windows, and steel wheels.
